About Us

This venture was started on 21st October 2020 in Singapore during the peak COVID-19 crisis , to help the needy globally. Our work includes supporting the needy and poor by various ways in providing them job opportunities , helping on settling their debts, their medical bills ,school fees, and feeding the hunger every week.

Focus Areas

We help your cause get discovered among our community of trusted providers across the globe who are ready to help. Although we invite all types of concerns we have some focus areas which are most frequently addressed with lesser turnaround times.

Medical Assistance

Emergency medical assistance is provided on successful case validation and supportless cases. Recommend to always subscribe for state medi support in respective states.

Education

Respective scholarship schemes and trust referral is made with endorsement to avail facilities , rare case after vetted validation support provided to fraction of cases.

Groceries

Monthly groceries support is provided on rare special case for short periods of time after successful case validation. Package include basket of food items for daily family needs.

Marriage Cause

Simple marriage cause supported on handpicked applications.

Settling Debts

Debt ridden single mother family is considered for support and beautiful loan is extended based on need only to specific group.

Rental and Roof

Many rental arrear cases considered during pandemic state situation due to jobless state , rare case roof support is provided.
